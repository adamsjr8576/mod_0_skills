1. how to become a Turing student
  1.1 research
    1.1.1 figure out criteria
      1.1.1.1 what length of program are you looking for?
      1.1.1.2 what type of school/degree do you want?
      1.1.1.3 do you want full time or part time?
      1.1.1.4 where do you want to be?
    1.1.2 google searching
      1.1.2.1 search coding bootcamps in Colorado
        1.1.2.1.1 make list containing top 5 of interest
          1.1.2.1.1.1 compare programs on list to criteria
          1.1.2.1.1.2 figure out contact info for each program on list
          1.1.2.1.1.3 send request for more information from each
      1.1.2.2 search coding degree options
        1.1.2.2.1 make list of options
          1.1.2.2.1.1 compare list to criteria
          1.1.2.2.1.2 narrow list down to degree option that fits best
      1.1.2.3 make list of top 3 options
    1.1.3 personal references
      1.1.3.1 make list of personal connection you have that could provide insight
        1.1.3.1.1 add/find contact info for people on the list
      1.1.3.2 reach out to each person on list
        1.1.3.2.1 get their feedback and input
        1.1.3.2.2 take notes during meetings
        1.1.3.2.3 reflect on feedback received
    1.1.4 reach out to programs
      1.1.4.1 schedule meeting/phone call with program/school
        1.1.4.1.1 prep questions and goals for meeting
        1.1.4.1.2 attend meeting/phone call
        1.1.3.1.3 take good notes
      1.1.4.2 reflect on info received from each program
    1.1.5 make decision
      1.1.5.1 Choose Turing because it appears to be the best fit!
  1.2 Try Coding weekend
    1.2.1 register for try coding weekend
      1.2.1.1 find try coding dates on their website
        1.2.1.1.1 figure out which dates works best with your schedule
        1.2.1.1.2 register for dates that work best with your schedule
          1.2.1.1.2.1 add event to your calendar
    1.2.2 prep for Try Coding weekend
      1.2.2.1 make list of things you want to get out of the weekend
      1.2.2.2 make list of questions you have about the program
      1.2.2.3 bring necessary tools
        1.2.2.3.1 bring pen/pencil
        1.2.2.3.2 bring notebook
        1.2.2.3.3 bring computer
        1.2.2.3.4 bring your A-game!
    1.2.3 attend Try Coding weekend
      1.2.3.1 take notes
      1.2.3.2 ask questions
      1.2.3.3 pay attention!
  1.3 apply
    1.3.1 fill out application
      1.3.1.1 go to turing.io
        1.3.1.1.1 select programs
        1.3.1.1.2 select Apply Now under either FEE or BEE
          1.3.1.1.2.1 log in using either Facebook or GitHub
          1.3.1.1.2.2 start application
          1.3.1.1.2.3 finish and submit application
    1.3.2 Get accepted to next interview round
    1.3.3 interview
      1.3.3.1 schedule time for interview
        1.3.3.1.1 use Turing resources to schedule interview time that works
          1.3.3.1.1.1 add to calendar
      1.3.3.2 prep for interview
        1.3.3.2.1 practice logic questions
          1.3.3.2.1.1 LSAT practice questions are a good resources
          1.3.3.2.1.2 practice at least 20
        1.3.3.2.2 list questions you still have about the program
        1.3.3.2.3 bring appropiate supplies
         1.3.3.2.3.1 bring pen
         1.3.3.2.3.2 bring notebook
      1.3.3.3 crush interview
        1.3.3.3.1 be yourself and nail it!
    1.3.4 accepted!
  1.4 enroll
    1.4.1 figure out what cohort you want to be in
      1.4.1.1 figure out what start date works best with your schedule
        1.4.1.1.1 don't forget to consider Mod 0 dates
    1.4.2 follow enrollment steps provided to you
      1.4.2.1 follow steps to enroll in decided cohort
      1.4.2.2 enroll in recommended Mod 0 course
    1.4.3 begin to consider logistics
      1.4.3.1 financials - tuition
      1.4.3.2 current job situation
      1.4.3.3 health insurance
      1.4.3.4 living situation
    1.4.4 schedule initial phone call to discuss logistics
      1.4.4.1 schedule phone call that works with your schedule
        1.4.4.1.1 add to calendar
      1.4.4.2 attend phone call
        1.4.4.2.1 take notes
        1.4.4.2.2 ask questions
  1.5 begin!
    1.5.1 GOOD LUCK!
